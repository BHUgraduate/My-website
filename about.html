About page

A polished structure for a serious website.

This layout keeps the website understandable and elegant. The idea is to make the UI feel refined, while leaving space for your Sanskrit, Kashi, AI, or learning content.

Design principle

Simple first

Good design is not about adding more things. It is about making the right things easier to see and use.

Motion principle

Animated but calm

The motion stays gentle so the site feels premium, not noisy. That helps the content stay readable.

Navigation principle

Separate pages

The tabs lead to different HTML files. That is cleaner for scaling than putting everything in one long page.

Future-ready

Easy to extend

Later, you can add more pages like AI, Translator, Lessons, Blog, Login, or Dashboard.
